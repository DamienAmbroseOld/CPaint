F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

PAINT can be accessed by clicking here.

What can I do in the editor?

You can tweak your brush with opacity, hue, colors, width and more! You can even upload an image and customise that one. You can also export it to 3 different file formats just in the push of a button.

How many formats are there?

You can save you drawing in .png, .jpeg, and .bmp.

What are the dimensions?

The maximum dimensions are 850x510px.
